Frequently asked questions

What causes sink drains to clog?

Common causes of sink drain clogs include grease buildup, food particles, hair, and foreign objects. Regular maintenance and prompt repairs can help prevent these issues.

How much does sink drain repair cost in Hayward, CA?

The cost of sink drain repair in Hayward, CA varies based on the severity of the issue. On average, you can expect to pay between $150 and $500, depending on the complexity of the repair.

Can I unclog a sink drain myself?

While some minor clogs can be handled with DIY methods, such as using a plunger or a plumber's snake, it's often best to consult professionals for persistent issues to avoid further damage.

What are the signs I need sink drain repair?

Signs that you may need sink drain repair include slow drainage, unusual odors, gurgling sounds, or water pooling around the sink. If you notice any of these, contact us for a professional assessment.
