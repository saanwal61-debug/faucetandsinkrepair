When To Call

When to call for faucet and sink repair

Recognizing the signs that you need faucet and sink repair is crucial for maintaining your plumbing system. If you notice any of the following symptoms, it's time to reach out for professional help.

1. Water pooling around the faucet

This indicates a possible leak that could lead to water damage if not addressed promptly.

2. Dripping water from the faucet

A dripping faucet is often a sign of a worn-out cartridge or seal, which can waste water and increase your bill.

3. Low water pressure in the sink

This could be due to mineral buildup or a problem with the plumbing, requiring immediate attention.

4. Unpleasant odors from the sink

Foul smells may indicate a clog or buildup in the drain, which can lead to more serious plumbing issues.

5. Frequent clogs in the sink

Recurring clogs suggest a deeper issue with your plumbing, necessitating a professional evaluation.
